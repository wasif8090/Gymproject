Bodybulding

Bodybuilding is a form of strength training focused on building muscle size, and symmetry through weightlifting. It involves progressive resistance training to stimulate muscle growth, combined with a high-protein diet and balanced nutrients to fuel the body. Bodybuilders often go through "bulking" phases to gain mass and "cutting" phases to reduce body fat, aiming for a lean and sculpted physique.The participants judged on muscle size, and aesthetics in competitions.
